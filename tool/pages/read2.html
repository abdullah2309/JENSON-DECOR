WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN A TABLE LAMP AND A BEDSIDE LAMP?


While the terms "table lamp" and "bedside lamp" are often used interchangeably, there are some differences between the two. Bedside lamps are typically smaller and shorter in height than table lamps, as they are designed to be used on a nightstand or bedside table. Table lamps, on the other hand, can come in a variety of sizes and are often used for decorative purposes in living rooms and other spaces.
